14 March. A morning: In bed until half past eleven. Jumble of thoughts which
slowly takes shape and hardens in an incredible fashion. Read in the afternoon
(Gogol, essay on the lyric), in the evening a walk, part of the time the defensible
but untrustworthy ideas of the morning in my head. Was in Chotek Park. Most
beautiful spot in Prague. Birds sang, the Castle with its arcade, the old trees
hung with last year's foliage, the dim light. Later Ottla arrived with D.
