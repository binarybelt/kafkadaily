14 October. The little street began with the wall of a graveyard on the one side
and a low house with a balcony on the other. In the house lived the pensioned
official, Friedrich Munch, and his sister, Elizabeth. 
A herd of horses broke out of the enclosure. 
Two friends went for a morning ride. 
‘Devils, save me from this benightedness!' shouted an old merchant who had
wearily lain down on the sofa in the evening and now, in the night, got up with
difficulty only by calling upon all his strength. There was a hollow knock at the
door. ‘Come in, come in, everything that is outside I' he shouted.
