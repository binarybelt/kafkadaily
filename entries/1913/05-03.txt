3 May. The terrible uncertainty of my inner existence. 
How I unbutton my vest to show Mr B. my rash. How I beckon him into
another room. 
The leper and his wife. The way her behind - she is lying in bed on her belly -
keeps rising up with all its ulcers again and again although a guest is present.
The way her husband keeps shouting at her to keep covered.
