26 February. I grant - to whom do I grant it? the letter? - that possibilities exist
in me, possibilities close at hand that I don't yet know of; only to find the way to
them! and when I have found it, to dare! This signifies a great many things:
that possibilities do exist; it even signifies that a scoundrel can become an
honest man, a man happy in his honesty. 
Your drowsy fantasies recently.
