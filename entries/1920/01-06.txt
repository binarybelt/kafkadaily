6 January. His every action seems extraordinarily new to him. If it had not this
fresh and living quality, of itself it would inevitably be something out of the old
swamp of hell, this he knows. But this freshness deceives him: it allows him to
forget his knowledge, or be heedless of it, or, though he see through the
freshness, see without pain. 
Today is undoubtedly the day, is it not, on which progress prepares to
progress farther?
