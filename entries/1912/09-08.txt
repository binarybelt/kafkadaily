8 September. Sunday morning. Yesterday a letter to Dr Schiller. 
Afternoon. The way my mother, together with a crowd of women, with a very
loud voice, is playing with some small children near by and drives me out of the
house. Don't cry! Don't cry! etc. That's his! That's his! etc. Two big people! etc.
He doesn't want to! … But! But! … How did you like Vienna, Dolphi? Was it nice
there? … I ask you, just look at his hands!
